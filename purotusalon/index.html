Purotu Salon & Boutique

ABOUT

Purotu means beautiful in Tahitian. Purotu's objective is to be recognized as the beauty salon that provides the finest products and personalized service.


Purotu offers a paradox of open space and intimacy. Located in Frenchtown, New Jersey, the salon not only provides a comfortable atmosphere inside for our clients but a wonderful outside experience with all the tremendous shops and restaurants next door.
